Who I am

I was born and raised in Muscat, Oman, where I first discovered my interest in technology and design. Growing up in a multicultural environment shaped my perspective and fueled my curiosity about how digital experiences are built. Over time, this curiosity turned into a passion for creating interfaces that are both visually appealing and intuitive for users.

Today, I am a college student at Trinity University of Asia, pursuing a degree in Software Engineering. I’m committed to developing my skills in frontend development, design, and problem-solving as I work toward becoming a versatile and innovative creator in the tech field. Every project I take on is an opportunity to learn, improve, and bring ideas to life through thoughtful and purposeful design.
